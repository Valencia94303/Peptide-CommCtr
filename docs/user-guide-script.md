# Peptide Command Center - User Guide Script

**Format:** Voiceover for screen recording or AI-generated video
**Length:** ~2 minutes
**Tone:** Friendly, casual, encouraging

---

## SCRIPT

Hey! Welcome to the Peptide Command Center. This is your daily co-pilot for tracking shots, macros, and workouts. Let me walk you through it real quick.

**[Show: Login screen]**

First, open the app and create your account. Use the exact display name you were given — this is important because the app automatically loads your personalized protocol, targets, and schedule based on your name.

**[Show: Dashboard - protocol card]**

This is your dashboard. Every day, you'll see exactly what you need to do. The app follows a simple rule: you either have morning shots or evening shots — never both on the same day.

On morning days, your shots are taken fasted — water only. Inject about thirty minutes before you work out, and don't eat for at least an hour after.

On evening days, take your shot at bedtime, ideally two hours after your last bite. But here's the thing — if you eat late, don't stay up waiting. Take the shot and go to sleep. Rest beats perfect timing.

**[Show: Tapping a shot button, it turns green]**

To log a shot, just tap the button. It turns green with a checkmark. The number on the right? That's exactly how many units to dial on your pen. The app calculates it automatically from your pen's concentration. No math required.

**[Show: Macro tracker with progress bars]**

Scroll down and you'll see your daily tracker. Calories, protein, and water — each with a progress bar that fills up as you hit your targets. These are your safety floors, not ceilings. Hit them every day.

For weight, the placeholder says it all: wake up, bathroom, scale — every day. Same conditions, same time. That's what makes the data useful.

**[Show: Workout card, tap complete, fireworks]**

Your workout section shows exactly what to do today. When you're done, tap the button and... yeah, you earned that. The app celebrates with you.

**[Show: Date navigator arrows]**

Forgot to log yesterday? No problem. Use the arrows at the top to go back to any previous day and fill in what you missed. Tap "Jump to Today" to come back.

**[Show: No-Go foods modal]**

See that red icon in the header? That's your No-Go list. Check it before ordering food. If it's on the list, skip it. Your body will thank you.

**[Show: Settings screen]**

In Settings, you can see your full profile, export your data, and find the full written User Guide if you ever need a refresher.

**[Show: Dashboard again]**

That's it. Open the app, log your shots, track your food, crush your workout. Every day you show up is a day closer to the goal.

Let's get it.
