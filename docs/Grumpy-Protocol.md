## The Stall Rule

The app does NOT automatically suggest a dose increase. All three conditions must be true for 3 consecutive weeks before alerting:

1. **Weight**: Less than 0.5% body weight loss per week for 3 weeks
2. **Compliance**: 100% of MOTS-c AND Wolverine shots logged during that period
3. **Protein**: 210g daily floor consistently met

If all three: "Metabolic Stall Detected. Consider 0.5mg Reta increase."

Only increase in 0.5mg or 1mg increments (e.g., 1.0mg to 1.5mg, not 1.0mg to 3.0mg).
