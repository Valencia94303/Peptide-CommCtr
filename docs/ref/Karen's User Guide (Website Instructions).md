## 📱 Karen’s Peptide & Treadmill Guide (The 15lb Goal)

Welcome to your personalized dashboard! This tool is designed to help us hit your 115 lb goal while protecting your energy, skin, and hair.

  

1. The Daily "Check-In"

  

• Log Your Shot: On the main screen, you’ll see your daily peptide (Tirzepatide on Mondays, MOTS-c on Tuesdays/Thursdays, etc.). Tap the "Log Shot" button once finished.

  

• Weight & Macros (Optional): Targets are 1,200 calories and 90g–100g of protein. These are your "safety floors" to keep you feeling good!

  

2. Your Treadmill Strategy (8lb Vest)

  

• The Goal: 3–5 sessions per week.

  

• The Rule: DO NOT HOLD THE HANDRAILS. Keeping your arms moving naturally with the 8lb vest is what tones your core and glutes.

  

• The Progress:

  

  • Weeks 1–2: 20 Mins | 2% Incline | 2.8–3.0 mph.

  

  • Weeks 3–6: 30 Mins | 5% Incline | 3.0 mph.

  

  • Weeks 7+: 30–40 Mins | 6–10% Incline | 3.0 mph.

  

3. The "No-Go" Button

  

Check the "No-Go List" on your dashboard before ordering Japanese or Mexican food.

  

• Avoid: Fried tempura, crispy tacos, and STRICTLY NO SPINACH.

  

• Focus: Sashimi, Miso, Grilled Chicken (Pollo Asado), and Ceviche.

  

4. Safety First

  

• Water: Aim for 80oz a day.

  

• The "Slow & Steady" Rule: We are aiming for 0.5 – 1 lb per week. If the app says "Warning: Losing too fast," we need to add a high-protein snack.
