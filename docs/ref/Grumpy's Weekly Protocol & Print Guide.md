## 💡 Important Timing & Fasting Notes

Morning Shots (Fasted)

  

• MOTS-c: Must be strictly fasted (water only). Take 30 mins before activity. Do not eat for at least 1 hour after.

  

• Reta (Weekly): Take fasted on Monday mornings to set your appetite control for the week.

  

Evening Shots (Bedtime)

  

• Diamond Glow: Ideally taken 2 hours after your last bite of food.

  

• Late Meal Rule: If you eat late, do not stay up just to hit the 2-hour window. Take the shot and go to sleep—rest is more important than perfect timing.

• Calisthenics: 4-week rotating bodyweight circuit.

  

• Rotation: Ensure you rotate injection sites daily (use a "clock" pattern around the navel) to prevent irritation from the Copper peptide.
