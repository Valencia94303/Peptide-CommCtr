🗓️ Injection Timing & Fasting Rules

  

Morning (Fasted)

  

Days: Mon, Tue, Thu, Sat

  

• MOTS-c / Wolverine / Reta: Must be strictly fasted (water only).

  

• Action: Take 30 mins before activity.

  

• Note: Do not eat for at least 1 hour after injection.

  

Evening (Bedtime)

  

Days: Wed, Fri, Sun

  

• Diamond Glow: Take on Recovery Days.

  

• Timing: Ideally 2 hours after your last bite.

  

• Late Meal Rule: If eating late, do not stay up. Take the shot and sleep—rest is the priority.

📋 Mixing Instructions (Insulin Pens)

  

1. Glow Pen: Mix 70mg vial with 7ml water (Concentrate). Transfer 1.5ml concentrate + 1.5ml fresh water into the 3ml pen cartridge.

  

2. Wolverine Pen: Mix 10mg vial with 3ml water.

  

3. Storage: Keep all reconstituted pens/vials in the dedicated fridge section.
