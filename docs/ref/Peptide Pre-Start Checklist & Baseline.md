## Peptide Pre-Start Checklist

Supplies Needed:

  

• Insulin Syringes: 1ml (100 unit) are best for math accuracy.

  

• Bacteriostatic Water: For reconstitution.

  

• Alcohol Swabs: For vial tops and injection sites.

  

• Sharps Container: For safe disposal.

  

• Insulin Pen:  1ml (100 unit) are best for daily shots.

  

Preparation Steps:

  

1. Refrigeration: Ensure a dedicated spot in the fridge for peptides once mixed.

  

2. Peptide Math: Confirm units per dose based on your vial MG (e.g., 5mg Reta vs 10mg MOTS-c).

  

3. Protein Prep: Have high-protein staples (chicken, eggs, Greek yogurt) ready for your 190g–210g daily target.

  

4. Hydration: Get a 120oz water bottle or a way to track intake.
