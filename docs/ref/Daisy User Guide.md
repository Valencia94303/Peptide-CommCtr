## 🧪 Your "Fab Three" Peptides

You are using a specialized stack shared from the Master Inventory. Here is the "Why" behind each one:

### **1. Tirzepatide (The Metabolic Anchor)**

- **What:** A GLP-1/GIP co-agonist.
    
- **Why:** It stabilizes your blood sugar and turns off the "food noise" (cravings).
    
- **The Goal:** It allows you to hit your 150lb goal without feeling like you are starving, especially with your high activity level.
    

### **2. MOTS-c (The Exercise Powerhouse)**

- **What:** A mitochondrial-derived peptide.
    
- **Why:** This is why your shots are **4x a week (Mon/Tue/Thu/Sat)**. MOTS-c mimics the signals of a hard workout. Since you are walking over 5 miles a day, MOTS-c ensures your mitochondria (your cell's batteries) are firing at 100%.
    
- **Why 4x vs. 1x?** Unlike the GLP-1 which stays in your system for a week, MOTS-c is a "pulse." We pulse it on your most active days to maximize fat burning.
    

### **3. Diamond Glow / GHK-Cu (The Recovery Shield)**

- **What:** A natural copper peptide.
    
- **Why:** You are training with a **10lb vest**. That puts extra stress on your skin and connective tissue.
    
- **The Goal:** We use this **3x a week (Wed/Fri/Sun evenings)** to tighten skin as the weight comes off and to repair your "internal scaffolding" while you sleep.
