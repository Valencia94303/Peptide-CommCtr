## 💡 Pro-Tips for the Fridge

• Pressure: When adding water to a 2ml vial, let the air pressure equalize by leaving the needle in for 10 seconds.

  

• Dilution: Using a 1:1 or 1:2 dilution in the 3ml pens drastically reduces the "Glow Sting."

  

• Manual Override: In the Admin Lab app, always use the Pen Setting Math (e.g., 70mg/14ml) to keep the unit clicks accurate.
