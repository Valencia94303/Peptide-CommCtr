## 💡 Timing & Fasting Protocols

Fasted Morning Shots (Mon/Tue/Thu)

  

• MOTS-c: Must be strictly fasted (water only). Take 30 mins before activity. Do not eat for at least 1 hour after.

  

• Tirzepatide (Weekly): Take fasted on Monday mornings. If nausea occurs, move to Sunday night.

  

Evening Recovery Shots (Wed/Fri/Sun)

  

• Diamond Glow: 10 Units via Insulin Pen.

  

• Empty Stomach: Ideally 2 hours after your last bite.

  

• Late Meal Rule: If you eat late, do not stay up. Take the shot and sleep—rest is the priority.

## 🏋️ Exercise Protocol (The 8lb Vest)

• The Rule: DO NOT HOLD THE HANDRAILS. Keep arms moving naturally to tone core and glutes.

  

• Incline Walking:

  

  • Weeks 1-2: 2% Incline | 2.8-3.0 mph | 20 Mins.

  

  • Weeks 3-6: 5% Incline | 3.0 mph | 30 Mins.

  

• Frequency: 3–5 sessions per week.

## ⚠️ Safety & Food

• Water: Aim for 80oz daily.

  

• No-Go List: Avoid Fried Foods, Gassy Veggies, and STRICTLY NO SPINACH.

  

• Protein Floor: 90g–100g daily to protect hair and skin.

  

• Goal: 0.5 – 1.0 lb weight loss per week. If higher, increase protein.
