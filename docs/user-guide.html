1. Getting Started

Open the app URL in your phone's browser (Safari or Chrome both work). You can add it to your home screen for quick access.

Sign In / Create Account

If this is your first time, create an account with your email. If you already have one, sign in with the same credentials.

Important: Display Name

Your display name must be set to exactly "Karen". This is how the app knows which protocol to load for you. If the name does not match, your shots and schedule will not appear correctly.

Once signed in, the app automatically loads your personal protocol -- your peptides, schedule, doses, and targets are all pre-configured.

2. Your Daily Dashboard

Date Navigator

At the top of the dashboard you will see today's date with left and right arrow buttons. Use these to browse previous days and review what you logged. If you have navigated to a past date, tap "Jump to Today" to return to the current day.

Morning vs. Evening Protocol

Each day has exactly one injection window -- either morning or evening, never both. The dashboard shows only the shots that apply to that window:

  • Morning days (Mon, Tue, Thu, Sat) -- fasted morning shots. Evening section is empty.
  • Evening days (Wed, Fri, Sun) -- bedtime shots. Morning section is empty.

Logging a Shot

When it is time for your injection, you will see an amber-colored button for each peptide due that day. Tap the button to log it -- it turns green with a checkmark to confirm. If you tapped by mistake, tap the green button again to undo the log.

Dosing Pill

Next to each shot button you will see a small pill-shaped badge showing the number of units to dial on your pen. These are pre-calculated from your pen's concentration so you do not need to do any math. Just dial the number shown and inject.

Example: 30 units

Weight Logging

Log your weight every morning using the same routine:

  1. Wake up
  2. Use the bathroom
  3. Step on the scale

Do this every day, same conditions. Consistency matters more than any single number. The app uses your weight trend to track progress and trigger safety checks.

4. Your Treadmill Protocol

8 lb Weighted Vest Required

Wear the vest for every session. DO NOT HOLD THE HANDRAILS. Keep your arms swinging naturally -- this engages your core and glutes.

Treadmill walking only. Four sessions per week on Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday.

Phase Weeks Duration Incline
Baseline 1-2 20 min 2%
Progression 3-6 30 min 5%
Power Burn 7+ 30-40 min 6-10%

Speed stays at 2.8-3.0 mph across all phases. Incline is the primary progression variable.

7. Fasting Rules

Morning Shots (Mon, Tue, Thu, Sat)

1. Water only -- no food, no coffee with cream, nothing but plain water before your shot.
2. Inject 30 minutes before activity -- give the peptide time to absorb before hitting the treadmill.
3. Do not eat for 1 hour after injection -- let the peptide work uninterrupted.

Evening Shots (Wed, Fri, Sun)

1. 2 hours after your last bite -- ideally taken at bedtime after your food has settled.
2. Late Meal Rule -- if you ate late and the 2-hour window would keep you up, just take the shot and go to sleep. Rest is more important than perfect timing.

8. Safety

Target Weight Loss Rate

Your healthy target is 0.5 to 1.0 lb per week. The app tracks your trend and will alert you if something is off.

"Losing Too Fast" Warning

If the app shows this warning, it means you are losing more than 2 lbs per week. The fix is simple: add a high-protein snack (like a protein shake or Greek yogurt) to bring your daily intake up by about 150 calories.

Protein First Rule

Tirzepatide can reduce your appetite. On days when you do not feel like eating much, prioritize protein above everything else. A protein shake counts. The 90-100g daily floor protects your muscle, skin, and hair while you lose weight.

Why This Matters

Losing weight too quickly without enough protein can cause muscle loss, thinning skin, and hair shedding. The safety rails in the app are there to keep your results healthy and sustainable.
