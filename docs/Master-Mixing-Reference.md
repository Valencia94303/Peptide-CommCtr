## Zero-Shared Hardware Rule

Miguel and Karen have separate physical pens. A "Glow Pen" for Miguel is distinct from a "Glow Pen" for Karen. Each pen is tracked independently by pen ID in the app with its own concentration based on the specific dilution used when filling.

---

## Why Two-Step Mixing?

- **Pressure Management**: Forcing 3mL of water into a 2mL vial causes the rubber stopper to pop or spray. The 2mL-to-3mL transfer keeps pressure safe.
- **Preservation**: Peptides degrade faster when highly diluted. Keeping a master concentrate and only diluting what you need means the remaining supply stays potent longer in the fridge.
- **Dilution Benefit**: Using a 1:1 or 1:2 dilution in the 3mL pens reduces the "Glow Sting" from the copper peptide.

---

## Pro Tips

- **Pressure Equalization**: When adding water to a 2mL vial, leave the needle in for 10 seconds to let air pressure equalize.
- **Injection Site Rotation**: Use a "clock" pattern around the navel to prevent irritation from the copper peptide (Diamond Glow).
- **Pen Replacement**: Replace pen cartridges every 28 days for freshness, even if volume remains.
- **Storage**: Keep all reconstituted masters and pens in the dedicated fridge section.
